Styles

Make your service look and feel like Gambling Commission services.

If you are using the GC Prototype Kit or have the GC Frontend stylesheets and javascript included in your build, the coded examples in the Design System will not need any additional styling.

If you need to apply styles manually, you should still follow existing conventions. For example, do not assign new meanings to colours, do not change the style of buttons or adjust the thickness of borders on form inputs.
